# AI-Powered SEO Content Production – Research Repository

## Topic

**AI-powered** SEO content production for B2B SaaS.

This repo collects high-signal practitioners, their recent content, and raw material (YouTube transcripts, LinkedIn posts, and other resources) to later build a concrete playbook for scaling SEO content with AI while maintaining quality and rankings.

## Why this topic

AI is now embedded into every stage of SEO content production: research, outlining, drafting, optimization, and refreshing existing content. 
For B2B SaaS teams, the challenge is not “using AI to write more,” but designing workflows, guardrails, and QA so AI actually improves traffic, leads, and content quality instead of creating thin content. 

This topic has strong public content from SEOs, content leads, and tool builders who share real experiments, frameworks, and case studies.
## Expert selection criteria

I selected experts who:

- Actively run or advise SEO/content programs (agencies, SaaS companies, tools). 
- Publish detailed workflows, case studies, or experiments, not just theory.
- Share recent content (mostly 2024–2025), so insights match the current AI + SEO landscape. 
- Focus on combining AI with human editing, brand voice, and search intent, instead of fully-automated “AI blog spam.” 

The full list with annotations lives in [`/research/sources.md`](research/sources.md).

## Repository structure

- `research/sources.md` – 10 annotated experts with profiles, key pieces, and why they were chosen.  
- `research/youtube-transcripts/` – YouTube video transcripts organized by expert and video, with metadata headers.  
- `research/linkedin-posts/` – Curated recent LinkedIn posts organized by author and date.  
- `research/other/` – Additional articles, podcast notes, and reference material. 
- `scripts/fetch_youtube_transcript.py` – Simple Python helper script to fetch and save YouTube transcripts via API.

This research is designed so it can be turned into a practical playbook for a B2B SaaS marketing team.
